About Us

    The simplest way to book a bus ticket in Sri Lanka.

    ABC (Pvt) Ltd an innovative bus ticket booking platform aims to create a hassle-free and improved public transport system in Sri Lanka. Since our inception in 2018, we have been able to effectively capture the abundance of returns.

    Public transport, especially bus travel, is a hugely growing business in Sri Lanka and other countries. Therefore, we are working to provide a user-friendly interface for the passenger to book a seat while traveling. We use innovative mechanisms for bus ticket booking systems to effectively compile and maintain records for bus companies and regulatory bodies.
